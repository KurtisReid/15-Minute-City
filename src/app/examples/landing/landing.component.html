What is a 15 Minute city?

A 15 minute city is a wonderful place to be. If you live in a 15 minute city, you can get almost all your needs satisfied within a 15 minute walk from your house. Walking around is so much better than driving. This app seeks to show if your neighboorhood is a 15 minute city. It searches for grocery stores, transit, and parks within a 15 minute walk.
